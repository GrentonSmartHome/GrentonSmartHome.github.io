What's New

v5.15.01 (build 2517)

14 May 2025

  • Added new virtual objects:
    • Statistics - allows you to add measurement values ​​to statistics, taking into account their category and type. The measurement can be recorded as an average, continuous or differential value and processed in regular intervals (15 minutes).
    • StatisticsPulse - allows you to add measurement values ​​to statistics, taking into account their category and type. The measurement is recorded based on pulses and sent every 15 minutes.
  • Fixed CLU restart when assigning a value of a different type to the set value to a persistent user feature.
  • Fixed the behavior of the Switch / SwitchOn / SwitchOff methods when called sequentially with the Time parameter for DIMM and LEDRGB objects.
  • Fixed operation of the Hold method after calling the Switch method on the ballast connected to the DALI Controller DIN module.
  • Improved data presentation for the RxBuffer and TxBuffer features of the RS-232 Controller module.

v5.14.01 (build 2452)

8 January 2025

  • Added support for estimated energy consumption statistics for Grenton 2.0 modules.
  • Added support for real energy consumption statistics for RELAY 2HP and RELAY 4HP modules.
  • Added support for statistics from Multisensor, One Wire, Smart Panel (version V6 or higher), and Touch Panels (version 1.1.4 or higher).
  • Support for Smart Panel V7.
